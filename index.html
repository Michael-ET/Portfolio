About Me

The code...


I began my journey in code as a university student being introduced to C#. I was tasked with building unique tools for audio triggering & manipulation. During this time I had my first taste of the potential to express myself creatively whilst building tools that offer new possibilities to the user. At this stage, however, I lacked the foundational knowledge to understand languages and the theory behind it. Never the less, this experience instilled a curiosity of coding technology that would come to define my goals for the future.

My journey continued later in life as I commited to exploring code further. Since October 2020 I have been a practicing developer, self-teaching through online and bootcamp recources. Having now built foundational knowledge I am able to realise my creative visions and explore the further possibilities of building different applications. I am now seeking to develop myself and my code further in a professional setting.

I am currently working with HTML, CSS, Javascript, JQuery, JSX, React, Redux, VS-Code and GitHub Version control. My recent projects have also allowed me to become proficient in RESTful API's, UI design, debugging and CommandLine interaction.

The person...


At the core I am a creative person. I have an eye for detail in aesthetics and a passion for visionary design. As a computer musician I am technically minded and enjoy working on complex projects (often on a collaborative basis with peers). My temperament is friendly, engaging and optimistic. I enjoy being outgoing and building strong social bonds. My interests include art, history, literature, movies, gaming, cooking and more.

I have a varied working history and throughout it I've learnt how to perform effectively in many different capacities. My previous roles include, quality assurance, data analysis, administration, sales, FOH/reception, customer service and more.
